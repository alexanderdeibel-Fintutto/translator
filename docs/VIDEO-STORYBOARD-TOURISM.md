```
Interior of a touring coach bus driving along a scenic coastal road. Through the large windows, a stunning Mediterranean coastline is visible -- but the tourists aren't looking. A tour guide stands at the front of the bus with a microphone, speaking energetically in German. The camera pans through the bus: 48 seats, almost full. A group of Chinese tourists in the middle rows looks confused, one filming the scenery but ignoring the guide. A Korean couple puts in earbuds and plays music instead.
```
