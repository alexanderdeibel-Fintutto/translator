### CLIP 1: PROBLEM -- Guide wiederholt sich (5s)
**Timecode: 0:00 -- 0:04**

```
Documentary-style handheld shot. A male tour guide in his 50s stands in front of an ancient Greek temple ruin on a sunny Mediterranean hillside. He speaks in English, gesturing at crumbling columns. Cut to his group of 15 tourists: a Korean family looks blankly, a French couple whispers confused to each other, an Arab teenager scrolls his phone bored, a Brazilian woman squints trying to understand. The guide stops, sighs, and starts again in broken French. The Korean family still doesn't understand. He tries again in slower English with exaggerated hand gestures. Frustration is visible on his face. The tourists shift their weight, some look at watches. The disconnect is painfully clear -- passionate guide, disconnected audience. Warm Mediterranean sunlight, slightly desaturated to emphasize the frustration. Handheld documentary feel, shallow depth of field alternating between guide and confused tourists. 24fps cinematic, no text overlays.
```

### CLIP 5: OFFLINE IM WALD / RUINE (6s)
**Timecode: 0:21 -- 0:26**

```
Dramatic transition from bright sunlight to a dim, atmospheric ancient forest path. A hiking guide leads a small group of 8 tourists through dense old-growth forest with moss-covered ruins -- crumbling stone walls and arches overtaken by nature. Shafts of sunlight break through the canopy. The guide speaks about the medieval history of the ruins. Phone screens glow in the forest shadows. A visual cue shows the WiFi signal dropping to zero bars -- they are deep in nature, far from any cell tower. But the translation continues uninterrupted. The guide's phone creates a local hotspot, shown by a subtle blue pulse radiating outward from it. Each tourist's phone stays active, translations flowing. A close-up of one tourist's phone shows text updating in real-time despite no internet connection. The atmosphere is mystical -- ancient ruins, dappled forest light, the soft glow of technology enabling understanding in a place where no signal reaches. Cinematic wide-angle lens, shallow depth of field. Warm amber highlights, cool green forest shadows.
```
